I very much enjoy designing for a print format. There is a certain sense of satisfactions that comes from seeing the design I spent hours or days or weeks working on in its completion and being able to physically hold it.

My favorite cards to get at the holidays have always been photo cards. Getting to design my own photo cards is one of my favorite parts of the holiday season!
